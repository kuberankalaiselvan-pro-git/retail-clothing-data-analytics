# Power BI Dashboards

This folder contains key dashboard views created in Power BI to support retail performance and customer analytics.

The dashboards are designed with a consistent structure and interactive slicers to enable multi-dimensional exploration while maintaining clarity.

---

## Core Performance & Profitability

### Sales & Profitability KPIs
**File:** `sales_profitability_kpis.png`

Provides a high-level overview of business performance, including:
- Total sales
- Profitability indicators
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

This dashboard highlights the disconnect between sales growth and margin performance.

---

### Sales vs Margin Trend
**File:** `sales_vs_margin.png`

Visualizes how increasing sales volume does not necessarily translate into improved profitability, supporting deeper diagnostic analysis.

---

## Customer Analytics

### Customer Retention & Segmentation
**File:** `customer_retention_insights.png`

Focuses on customer behavior and loyalty by analyzing:
- RFM customer segmentation
- Retention rate trends
- New vs repeat customers
- Geographic distribution of sales

Reveals acquisition-led growth with weak long-term retention.

---

## Product & Inventory Health

### Category Margin Analysis
**File:** `category_margin_analysis.png`

Analyzes profitability at the product category level, identifying:
- High-margin “hero” categories
- Low-margin categories contributing to margin erosion
- Imbalances between revenue and profitability

---

## Time & Seasonality

### Seasonal & Time-Based Trends
**File:** `time_seasonality_trends.png`

Examines sales and performance patterns over time to identify:
- Seasonal fluctuations
- Quarterly trends
- Year-over-year behavior

Supports forecasting and planning decisions.
